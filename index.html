What is a MNsure Navigator?

MNsure Navigators are certified experts who provide free health insurance assistance to all Minnesotans. We work through trusted community organizations to help you find the coverage that's right for you.

Whether you need help with Medical Assistance, MinnesotaCare, or private insurance through the MNsure marketplace, our navigators guide you through every step of the process.

There are no eligibility requirements to use our services. We're here to help anyone who needs assistance understanding their health coverage options.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MNsure Navigators and health coverage

MNsure Navigators provide free, in-person assistance to help you apply for and enroll in health coverage. We help with Medical Assistance, MinnesotaCare, and private insurance through the MNsure marketplace. We also provide ongoing support after enrollment.
Navigator services are completely free. There is no charge for consultations, application assistance, or ongoing support. We're here to help all Minnesotans access health coverage.
Medical Assistance (MA) and MinnesotaCare are free or low-cost health programs for people with low incomes. MA is Minnesota's Medicaid program, and MinnesotaCare is a public health program for those who don't qualify for MA but still need affordable coverage.
